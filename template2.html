1. Make a Plan First

Start your day with a clear, actionable plan. Break tasks into priorities and set realistic deadlines to stay focused and avoid last-minute stress.

2. Time Discipline

Stick to your schedule and practice time discipline by allocating time for each task. Avoid distractions and respect deadlines to stay on track.

3. Take Care of Your Health

Physical and mental well-being are critical for productivity. Incorporate breaks, healthy eating, and exercise into your routine to stay focused and energized.
